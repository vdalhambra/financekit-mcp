# FinanceKit MCP — Pricing

FinanceKit is open source (MIT) and **free to self-host** with no functional limits. If you want a zero-setup, managed, always-up-to-date experience with premium features, use [MCPize](https://mcpize.com/mcp/financekit-mcp).

## Tiers

### Free — $0/month
**100 calls/month** — Perfect for trying it out.

Includes 5 basic tools:
- `stock_quote` — Current price and basics
- `multi_quote` — Batch quotes (up to 5 tickers)
- `company_info` — Company profile
- `crypto_price` — Single coin price
- `market_overview` — Major indices

### Hobby — $9/month
**2,500 calls/month** — For individual investors and learners.

All Free tools, plus:
- `crypto_trending` — Top trending coins
- `crypto_search` — Find coins
- `crypto_top_coins` — Top N by market cap
- `technical_analysis` — Basic indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger)
- `price_history` — Historical OHLCV

### Pro — $29/month ⭐ Most popular
**10,000 calls/month** — For active traders and developers.

Everything in Hobby, plus:
- `compare_assets` — Multi-asset comparison
- `portfolio_analysis` — Full portfolio metrics
- Extended technical indicators (ADX, Stochastic, ATR, OBV)
- Pattern detection (Golden Cross, Death Cross)
- Priority response times
- **Premium v1.2+ tools** (see below)

### Team — $79/month
**50,000 calls/month** — For small teams and funds.

Everything in Pro, plus:
- 5 team seats
- CSV export for all data
- Email support
- Shared watchlists

### Business — $179/month
**200,000 calls/month** — For serious trading operations.

Everything in Team, plus:
- Webhooks for price/indicator alerts
- Smart alerts (RSI cross, price targets)
- Backtesting engine (premium tool)
- SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
- Chat support

### Enterprise — $499/month
**Unlimited calls** — For hedge funds, fintech companies.

Everything in Business, plus:
- White-label branding
- On-premises deployment option
- Dedicated Slack channel
- Custom integrations
- Named support engineer
